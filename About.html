The Founder of dAyana Prince Art Works

(Awethu Amathemba Ayanda)

Founded in the late 2011s, dAyana Prince Art Works is a bespoke South African production studio serving the public. Operating in Gauteng and the KwaZulu Natal, and delivering Artworks only within South Africa.

With a formidable track record as a one woman Art Production studio, dAyana Art Works is distinguished by its interpersonal customer service. dAyana Art Works success is headed by a capable independent-minded artist.

dAyana Art Works is built on a service ethos that insuring that the customer is involved in extracting beauty in life and eventually representing that beauty in a nostalgic illustration of Art.
